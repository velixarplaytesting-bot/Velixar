About Velixar Playtesting

Velixar Playtesting is a professional game testing platform created for indie developers and studios that want to improve gameplay quality before launch.

We help developers collect real player feedback, identify bugs, improve balancing, and understand how gamers interact with their games.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help developers create polished and engaging gaming experiences through structured testing and organized reports.

Why Velixar?

Velixar focuses on professionalism, communication, and detailed testing feedback designed for modern indie development.
